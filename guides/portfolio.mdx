## Managing V3 positions

Click any V3 position to see its range, current price, and status.

- **In range** — Actively earning fees.
- **Out of range** — Not earning; sitting in one asset.

From the position view you can:

- **Collect fees** without touching the underlying liquidity.
- **Increase liquidity** to add more to the same range.
- **Remove liquidity** to withdraw partially or fully.

<Tip>
  To change a V3 range, remove the position and open a new one with your desired bounds.
</Tip>

## Points

Eligible activity (swaps, liquidity provision) can earn weekly points after you complete the one-time **points registration** signature. This is a free signature — no gas, no on-chain transaction.

Check your point total and current week's progress from the Portfolio page.

## Referrals

Share your referral link to earn a share of points from users you bring in. Your link and stats are on the Portfolio page.

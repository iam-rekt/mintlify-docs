Liquidity providers deposit assets that traders swap against. In return, they earn a share of that pool’s fees and take on the pool’s price risk.

RobinSwap supports two pool types: **V2** (simple) and **V3** (concentrated).

<Frame caption="The Liquidity directory compares pool size, 24-hour flow, LP fees, estimated APR, and age.">
  ![RobinSwap pools and liquidity view.](/images/Liquidity.png)
</Frame>

## Pick a pool style

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="V2 pools" icon="circle">
    Deposit two assets at the current pool ratio. Liquidity remains active across the full price curve.
  </Card>

  <Card title="V3 pools" icon="chart-simple">
    Set a **minimum** and **maximum** price. The position earns fees only while the market stays inside that range.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

<Tip>
  V2 is simpler to maintain. V3 gives you more control, but a narrower range can move out of range sooner.
</Tip>

## Create a position

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Liquidity">
    Choose **Add liquidity**, then select V2 or V3.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Pick your pair">
    Select two tokens. For a new pool, the first provider sets the starting price. Review it carefully.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter an amount">
    For a balanced deposit, enter one side and RobinSwap calculates the other from the pool and range.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Approve and deposit">
    For V3, drag or type the minimum and maximum. Use the chart zoom controls to inspect the surrounding market before confirming.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Approve and deposit">
    Approve the required tokens, then review the final amounts in your wallet.
  </Step>
</Steps>

V2 returns fungible LP tokens. V3 creates a position NFT containing the pair, fee tier, liquidity, and range.

## Single-token V3 deposit

Choose **One token** to zap a single asset into a V3 position. RobinSwap quotes the internal swap, checks the estimated impact, and deposits both sides into the selected range.

The zap fee is **0.15%** of the zap value. The quote shows the estimated position amounts before you sign.

<Warning>
  A zap is not a way around thin liquidity. Reduce the amount or widen the range if the protected quote cannot balance the position safely.
</Warning>

## How you earn

Every swap in a pool pays the pool's fee. That fee is split among LPs in proportion to their share of the active liquidity.

- **V2** — every LP earns on every swap, proportional to pool share.
- **V3** — only LPs whose range includes the current price earn from that swap. Fees accumulate as claimable balances.

<Tip>
  Fees don't auto-compound — they sit as claimable amounts until you collect or reinvest them.
</Tip>

## Adjust a V3 range

Open any position from **Pools** or **Portfolio** to manage it.

Open the position in Portfolio and choose **Edit range**. Pick new bounds and review the amounts. RobinSwap removes the old liquidity and mints the replacement position; wallets that support atomic batches can submit the flow together, while other wallets may show separate confirmations.

An out-of-range position is held in one asset and no longer earns fees. Moving the range can put it back to work, but it also changes your exposure.

## Zap out

Choose **Zap out** on a V3 position to remove the full position, collect its fees, and receive one selected asset. The zap-out fee is also **0.15%**. Native ETH can be selected when the output is WETH.

## Move existing liquidity

Portfolio can detect official Uniswap V3 positions held by the connected wallet. Uniswap V2 LP tokens can be found by entering the pair address.

The migration removes the source liquidity and deposits the available assets into the matching RobinSwap pool. RobinSwap charges **no migration fee**. Price protection can stop the transaction if the source and destination pools move too far apart.

## Claiming fees

Fees are separate from your deposited liquidity. You can collect them without reducing the position.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the position">
    Click any position on the **Pools** page or **Portfolio**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Check unclaimed fees">
    The card shows fees earned in each token since your last claim.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Collect">
    Click **Collect fees** and confirm in your wallet. Tokens land in your wallet.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Info>
  Use **Claim all** in Portfolio to collect from multiple eligible positions in one action when supported.
</Info>

---
title: "First swap"
description: "Connect a wallet, review a quote, and make a swap on Robinhood Chain."
---

<Info>
  Keep a small amount of ETH for gas. If your assets are on another supported network, the swap flow can route them into Robinhood Chain.
</Info>

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open RobinSwap">
    Go to [robinswap.finance](https://www.robinswap.finance) and select **Connect wallet**. Confirm the connection request in your wallet.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Switch to Robinhood Chain">
    For a local swap, use Robinhood Chain (chain ID `4663`). For a cross-chain swap, select the source network in the swap card.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Pick your tokens">
    Choose the token you are selling, enter an amount, then select the token you want to receive. Search by contract address when you are unsure—names and symbols can be copied.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Review the quote">
    Check the output, **Minimum received**, price impact, and selected route. The quote refreshes as market conditions change.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Approve and swap">
    ERC-20 tokens require an approval before the router can swap them. Confirm that transaction first, then confirm the swap. Native ETH does not require approval.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Check>
  A local swap finishes after its Robinhood Chain transaction confirms. A cross-chain swap stays pending until the destination transfer settles.
</Check>

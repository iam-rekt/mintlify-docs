---
title: "Weekly points and referrals"
description: "How RobinSwap records eligible activity and applies referral boosts."
---

RobinSwap points are an offchain participation program. There is no points token contract, no onchain claim balance, and no guarantee of future value.

## Register once

Connect the wallet you intend to use and sign the points registration message from the swap experience. The signature proves wallet control; it does not spend funds or grant token approvals.

## Eligible activity

The points service verifies confirmed RobinSwap transaction receipts against the configured factory contracts. The browser does not self-report action type, trade size, or a multiplier.

The current baseline model is:

| Activity | Base points |
| --- | ---: |
| Confirmed swap | 100 |
| Confirmed liquidity action | 250 |

Verified size can apply a weekly multiplier. Rules, thresholds, allocation totals, and dates are operator-controlled and can change for future weeks.

## Referral names

Set a unique referral name and share it with a new user. Referral attribution is one-time. The referred wallet can receive a points boost, while the referrer receives a points-only share of eligible invitee activity. No percentage of a user’s swap fee or LP fee is diverted through referrals.

## Weekly distribution

The program operator sets the total allocation for the week, then distributes it across verified activity under the active rules. Portfolio displays the recorded results after distribution.

<Warning>
  Do not sign messages you do not recognize. RobinSwap registration is a message signature only; a request to approve or transfer tokens is not points registration.
</Warning>

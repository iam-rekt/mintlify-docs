When you add liquidity, you deposit two tokens into a pool. Every time someone swaps through that pool, a fee is charged — and a share of that fee accrues to you.

RobinSwap supports two pool types: **V2** (simple) and **V3** (concentrated).

<Frame caption="The Pools view shows every position, its status, and unclaimed fees at a glance.">
  ![RobinSwap pools and liquidity view.](/images/Explore.png)
</Frame>

## V2 vs V3 — which one?

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="V2 pools" icon="circle">
    Deposit two tokens in equal value. Your liquidity spreads across all prices. Simple, passive, no management needed.
  </Card>

  <Card title="V3 pools" icon="chart-simple">
    Pick a **price range**. Your liquidity is concentrated there, earning more fees when the price is inside your range — and none when it's outside.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

<Tip>
  New to LP? Start with V2. Comfortable managing ranges? V3 can earn more on the same capital.
</Tip>

## Adding V2 liquidity

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Pools">
    Go to the **Pools** page and choose **New position**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Pick your pair">
    Select two tokens. If the pool doesn't exist yet, you'll be prompted to create it and set the initial price.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter an amount">
    Enter one side — the other fills automatically based on the current pool ratio.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Approve and deposit">
    Approve each token (one-time), then confirm the deposit.
  </Step>
</Steps>

You'll receive LP tokens representing your share of the pool.

## Adding V3 liquidity

<Steps>
  <Step title="Pick a fee tier">
    Fee tiers match volatility. Lower fees for stable pairs, higher fees for volatile ones.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Set your price range">
    Choose a **min** and **max** price. Your liquidity earns fees only while the market price is inside this range.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Deposit tokens">
    Deposit amounts of both tokens matching your chosen range. Narrower ranges = higher capital efficiency, but more time out-of-range.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Confirm">
    Approve and confirm. Your position appears in your portfolio.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## How you earn

Every swap in a pool pays the pool's fee. That fee is split among LPs in proportion to their share of the active liquidity.

- **V2** — every LP earns on every swap, proportional to pool share.
- **V3** — only LPs whose range includes the current price earn from that swap. Fees accumulate as claimable balances.

<Tip>
  Fees don't auto-compound — they sit as claimable amounts until you collect or reinvest them.
</Tip>

## Adjusting your position

Open any position from **Pools** or **Portfolio** to manage it.

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Add more liquidity">
    Deposit additional tokens into an existing V2 position, or add to the same range on a V3 position.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Remove liquidity">
    Withdraw partially (by percentage) or fully. Fully exiting a V3 position burns its NFT.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Change a V3 range">
    Ranges are fixed once created. To move a V3 range: **remove** the position, then **open a new one** with new min/max prices. Collect fees first so they don't get bundled into the withdrawal.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Claiming fees

Fees are separate from your deposited liquidity — you can claim them without touching the position.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the position">
    Click any position on the **Pools** page or **Portfolio**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Check unclaimed fees">
    The card shows fees earned in each token since your last claim.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Collect">
    Click **Collect fees** and confirm in your wallet. Tokens land in your wallet.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Info>
  Collecting fees costs gas. Batch collections rather than claiming tiny amounts frequently.
</Info>

RobinSwap points are an offchain participation program. They are not a token, an onchain balance, or a promise of future value.

## Join once

Connect the wallet you plan to use and sign the points registration message. This proves wallet control; it does not spend funds, approve tokens, or use gas.

## Eligible activity

Confirmed RobinSwap swaps and liquidity actions are verified from their onchain receipts. The browser cannot award itself points.

The current baseline model is:

| Activity | Base points |
| --- | ---: |
| Confirmed swap | 100 |
| Confirmed liquidity action | 250 |

Verified size can apply a multiplier. Active liquidity missions and referral boosts may add weight under that week’s rules.

## Referral names

Claim a unique referral name and share it with a new user. Attribution is applied once. Any referral reward is points-only; it does not divert swap or LP fees.

## Weekly distribution

Each week has a total allocation. The final distribution divides it across eligible wallets in proportion to their verified score under that week’s rules.

<Warning>
  Only sign messages you recognize. RobinSwap registration is a message signature; a request to approve or transfer tokens is not points registration.
</Warning>

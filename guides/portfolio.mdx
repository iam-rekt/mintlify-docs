## Managing V3 positions

Click any V3 position to see its range, current price, and status.

- **In range** — Actively earning fees.
- **Out of range** — Not earning; sitting in one asset.

From a V3 position card you can:

- **Collect fees** without touching the underlying liquidity.
- **Add liquidity** at the position's current fee tier and range.
- **Reduce / withdraw** part or all of the position into both pool assets.
- **Edit range** to move the capital into new bounds.
- **Change fee tier** to remint the position in another supported V3 pool tier.
- **Zap out** to withdraw, collect, and receive one asset.

## Bring liquidity over

The migration panel finds compatible Uniswap V3 positions automatically and supports Uniswap V2 lookup by pair address. The RobinSwap migration fee is none, and price-deviation protection applies.

Use **Fee Tier** to move a RobinSwap V3 position into a different tier. The flow can initialize a missing destination pool and creates a replacement NFT whose owner, tier, range, and liquidity are verified after confirmation.

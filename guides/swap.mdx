## Reading a quote

Every quote shows the key numbers you need before signing.

<Frame>
  ![RobinSwap swap interface showing a live quote.](/images/hero-1.png)
</Frame>

| Field | What it means |
| --- | --- |
| **Expected output** | The estimated amount you'll receive at current prices. |
| **Minimum received** | The lowest amount the trade will execute at, based on your slippage setting. |
| **Price impact** | How much your trade moves the pool price. Higher impact = worse rate. |
| **Route** | Where the swap executes and, for cross-chain trades, how it reaches Robinhood Chain. |
| **Network fee** | Gas paid on the network where you sign. It is separate from the amount received. |

## Slippage and quote freshness

Slippage is how much price movement you allow between quote and execution.

- **0.1%–0.5%** — Often appropriate for stable pairs and deep pools.
- **0.5%–1%** — A common starting point for ordinary trades.
- **Above 1%** — May be necessary for volatile or thin markets; review the risk carefully.

Set it too low and the trade may fail. Set it too high and you allow a worse fill. RobinSwap refreshes the quote automatically; review again if the route or minimum received changes.

## Approvals

An ERC-20 token needs an approval before the selected router can use it. Native ETH does not. Your wallet shows approvals separately from the swap transaction.

## Local confirmation

1. Review the quote and minimum received.
2. Click **Swap**.
3. Confirm in your wallet.
4. Wait for the Robinhood Chain confirmation.

Your new balance updates automatically once the transaction confirms.

## Cross-chain settlement

After the source transaction is submitted, the swap card keeps the route status visible. It shows a time estimate when one is available and moves through pending, destination submitted, and complete states.

<Info>
  Do not submit the same cross-chain trade again just because the destination balance has not arrived yet. Check the active status and source transaction first.
</Info>

## Troubleshooting

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Transaction failed">
    Usually one of: slippage too tight, insufficient gas, or the token has transfer restrictions. Try raising slippage slightly or reducing the amount.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="No route found">
    The selected pair, amount, or network path has no executable quote. Try a smaller amount, a common asset such as ETH, or another source network.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Quote keeps changing">
    Pool and bridge conditions move. RobinSwap refreshes the quote to show current execution terms; review the latest minimum before confirming.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

Swapping on RobinSwap is designed to be simple: pick two tokens, review the quote, confirm in your wallet.

<Frame caption="The swap card shows live quotes, price impact, and minimum received before you confirm.">
  ![RobinSwap swap interface.](/images/Explore.png)
</Frame>

## Before you swap

- Your wallet is connected and set to **Robinhood Chain** (chain ID `4663`).
- You have enough ETH for gas.
- If you're trading an ERC-20 token, RobinSwap will ask for a one-time **approval** before the first trade of that token.

## Reading a quote

Every quote shows the key numbers you need before signing.

<Frame>
  ![RobinSwap swap interface showing a live quote.](/images/Explore.png)
</Frame>

| Field | What it means |
| --- | --- |
| **Expected output** | The estimated amount you'll receive at current prices. |
| **Minimum received** | The lowest amount the trade will execute at, based on your slippage setting. |
| **Price impact** | How much your trade moves the pool price. Higher impact = worse rate. |
| **Route** | Which pool(s) your trade goes through. |
| **Network fee** | Gas paid to Robinhood Chain validators. |

<Tip>
  If price impact is high (\>1-2%), try a smaller amount or split the trade.
</Tip>

## Slippage

Slippage is how much price movement you'll accept between quote and execution. The default is usually fine.

- **0.1% - 0.5%** — Stable pairs and deep pools.
- **0.5% - 1%** — Most trades.
- **1%\+** — Volatile tokens or thin liquidity.

Set slippage too low and your trade may fail. Too high and you risk a worse fill.

## Approvals

The first time you trade an ERC-20 token, you'll sign an approval so the router can move it. This is a one-time step per token. Native ETH does not need approval.

## Confirming the swap

1. Review the quote and minimum received.
2. Click **Swap**.
3. Confirm in your wallet.
4. Wait for on-chain confirmation — usually seconds on Robinhood Chain.

Your new balance updates automatically once the transaction confirms.

## Troubleshooting

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Transaction failed">
    Usually one of: slippage too tight, insufficient gas, or the token has transfer restrictions. Try raising slippage slightly or reducing the amount.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="No route found">
    There's no pool with enough liquidity for this pair. Try a different token, smaller size, or route through a common asset like ETH.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Quote keeps changing">
    Prices move. RobinSwap refreshes the quote to reflect current pool state. Lock it in by confirming quickly.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

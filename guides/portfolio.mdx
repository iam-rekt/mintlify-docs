## Managing V3 positions

Click any V3 position to see its range, current price, and status.

- **In range** — Actively earning fees.
- **Out of range** — Not earning; sitting in one asset.

From a V3 position card you can:

- **Collect fees** without touching the underlying liquidity.
- **Edit range** to move the capital into new bounds.
- **Zap out** to withdraw, collect, and receive one asset.

<Tip>
  Position values are shown only when RobinSwap has a secure USD price for the underlying assets. An unpriced position still exists onchain even when the interface shows a dash.
</Tip>

## Bring liquidity over

The migration panel finds compatible Uniswap V3 positions automatically and supports Uniswap V2 lookup by pair address. Migration has no RobinSwap migration fee and includes price-deviation protection.

## Points and referrals

Eligible activity can be recorded for weekly points only after you complete the one-time **points registration** signature. It is a wallet signature, not an approval or onchain transaction.

Check your point total and current week's progress from the Portfolio page.

## Referrals

Referral attribution is set once for a new wallet. Referral rewards, eligibility, and weekly allocations are program rules, not a share of a user’s trading or LP fees.

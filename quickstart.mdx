<Info>
  You need a wallet with Robinhood Chain enabled and enough ETH for gas plus the token you want to trade.
</Info>

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open RobinSwap and connect">
    Go to [robinswap.finance](https://www.robinswap.finance) and choose **Connect wallet**. RobinSwap only reads your address after you approve.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Switch to Robinhood Chain">
    The app defaults to Robinhood Chain (chain ID `4663`). If your wallet is on another network, accept the switch prompt.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Pick your tokens">
    Choose the **From** token and enter an amount, then choose the **To** token. You can search by symbol, name, or contract address. Always double-check imported token addresses.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Review the quote">
    Check the expected output and **Minimum received**. The quote refreshes automatically as prices move.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Approve and swap">
    ERC-20 tokens need a one-time approval. Confirm the approval, then confirm the swap in your wallet. Native ETH skips the approval step.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Check>
  When the transaction confirms, your new token balance appears in your wallet. Eligible activity counts toward weekly points once you complete the one-time points signature.
</Check>
